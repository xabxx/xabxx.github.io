Hello, World.

Posted on July 18th, 2017

 

 

 

 

I am getting stuck on words, I don't know where to start. Writing this very first post is almost like starting a new chapter in my life, I feel the excitement for the unknown, but it also comes with fears. This site serves one purpose and one purpose only - to make myself google-able. But it's far to silly to be a reason, and I hope here will soon turn into something meaningful, like a diary or a pink ribbon, or a secret, or something (idrc).

Dreams

"What do you want to do when you grow up?" I actually prefer asking myself "What do you want to do while you are aging?" That's right, I am not going to "grow" anymore, my brain cells die at a rate I can't afford, wrinkles are climbing on my face, and I need less sleep than before (that's a lie). I start to value my time, start to think if doing X is worth of my time. My dreams are cloudy, I want to learn and do everything, though I know I can never pick the stars in the sky. On the extreme contrary, I also sort of just want to do all kinds of drugs and die next to a trashcan, I swear I can picture that life. But I have gone too far to throw everything away, to not live a socially acceptable life, to not make efforts towards some supposedly great goals, and to not strive for what most people called "success". Life is a bore, I was forced to live it the day my parents decided to have unprotected intercourse. Don't be mistaken, I am grateful to this life, or how was I supposed to find out the cliche in all human stories? :)

Be true to youself.

Yeh I just quoted myself.

I am out of ideas to write more bs here. You, have a good life okay? Let's pray that cancer cure we all need will be invented soon. Bye now.
